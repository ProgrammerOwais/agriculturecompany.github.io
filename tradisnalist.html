How to win the absentee operator

HOW TO SUPPORT THEM ALONG THE JOURNEY

Absentee Operators do not want to put too much energy into the purchase process.
They put a lot of trust into their supplier to offer guidance on the right solution for them – and reward it with loyalty if the entire process goes smoothly.

Value proposition

Proven technology that supports operational efficiency and helpsto sustain the business (e.g., use case studies, benchmarks, TCO/ROI calculators to demonstrate impact; consider “no downtime” promise/offering incl. parts availability, quick service)

Standard solution that make financial sense. (ROI calculation increased net income ) Trusted supplier that ensures smooth process.
