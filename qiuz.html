1. Which segment is looking for proven technology to drive efficiency?

Satisfied Traditionalist

Satisfied Traditionalists are looking for simple solutions rather than technological solutions. Their focus lies more on the reliability and dependability and they only have a moderate concern with operational cost. In contrast, Efficiency Masters are more open to technological solutions and their key concern is operational efficiency.

Efficiency MastersCorrect answer

Correct! Efficiency masters tend to be interested in technological solutions that drive efficiency. Though, they typically are not early adopters and would like to have the actual impact of the technology demonstrated (e.g.in case studies, testimonials, using TCO calculators).

Absentee Operator

Absentee Operators tend to look more for simple rather than technological solutions. As our products often are “one cog in a big machine” for their business operations, they direct focus their efforts to maximize operational efficiency on other parts of their operation. They do not want to put too much energy into the purchase process and are not too involved with our product portfolio. In contrast, Efficiency Masters are more open to technological solutions and their key concern is operational efficiency.

Sophisticate

Sophisticates are indeed interested in technological solutions. However, they tend to be early adopters taking pride using latest and greatest technology - rather than employing proven technology with the primary benefit of improving operational efficiency. Their focus lies more on quality output than operational efficiency, which is the key motivation for the Efficiency Master.
