THE ABSENTEE OPERATOR

Look for a smooth process and simple solutions matching their budget

We would like this to be a “no brainer” decision. We are not looking for anything too complex. Ideally, we have already worked with the supplier before, and everything went smoothly.

KEY CHARACTERISTICS

Decision makers typically more removed from operations

Equipment is perceived almost as a commodity

They often already have a rough budget in mind
