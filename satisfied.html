SATISFIED TRADITIONALISTS

Look for standard, reliable and dependable solutions

It just needs to work – nothing fancy. We need to be able to operate and maintain it. It needs to be reliable and dependable. We’ll be talking to a few guys, so we appreciate help in comparing their offers.

KEY CHARACTERISTICS

They take a stable approach to the business

Look for simple, standard solutions – and have a low tech orientation

Hands-on, informal, pragmatic, and overall quite content

Least supplier loyal with the highest number of brands

How to win the absentee operator

HOW TO SUPPORT THEM ALONG THE JOURNEY

Absentee Operators do not want to put too much energy into the purchase process.
They put a lot of trust into their supplier to offer guidance on the right solution for them – and reward it with loyalty if the entire process goes smoothly.

Value proposition

Proven technology that supports operational efficiency and helpsto sustain the business (e.g., use case studies, benchmarks, TCO/ROI calculators to demonstrate impact; consider “no downtime” promise/offering incl. parts availability, quick service)

Support the process

Stand out of the crowd” in supporting the selection process (fit with needs, comparability of products)

Service opportunity

Emphasize value-added services (remote monitoring , performance , optimization) and operatons consultation; consider contract model for equipment renewal and maintenance. ) Trusted supplier that ensures smooth process.
